About

Hayley Lauren is a textile designer and artist based in Melbourne, Australia. She is in her final year of Bachelor of Arts (Textile Design) at RMIT University majoring in constructed textiles.

Hayley’s art and designs come in a variety of styles and mediums. From watercolour to organic line work, to stencils cuts and detailed wildlife drawings. Her free flowing designs are a creative expression and insight into the way she sees the world. “For me, it’s about being free and having fun with art.”

Hayley has collaborated with companies such as Uppercup, Soxy Beast and Borne Bespoke. Projects have included patterned reusable coffee cups, knitted sock designs and fabric designs for pocket tees.

Hayley is constantly creating and on the lookout for collaborations and new projects.
